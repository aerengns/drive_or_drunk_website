Drive or Drunk

Smart and socially responsible ride-sharing for nightlife

Connect sober drivers with people who need safe transportation after consuming alcohol. A community-driven platform that promotes safety and social responsibility.

Learn More

Key Features

Find Available Drivers

Locate sober drivers nearby who can provide safe transportation to your destination.

Local Event Calendar

Browse events in your area and find drivers specifically available for those events.

Event Mapping

Visualize events and available drivers on an interactive map for easy planning.

In-App Messaging

Coordinate ride details directly with drivers through the integrated chat system.

Reviews & Ratings

Rate your experience and read reviews to ensure quality and trustworthiness.

User Verification

Verified drivers through ID card verification and facial recognition technology.
